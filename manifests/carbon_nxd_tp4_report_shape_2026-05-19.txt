Notes
- Carbon's custom tokenizer must be loaded with trust_remote_code=true.
- Mixed-base raw DNA may not use the compact 6-mer path automatically; use explicit <dna> mode.
- Carbon-500M and Carbon-8B run under TP=4, but emit NxDI's GQA-to-MHA fallback warning.
- Carbon-3B is cleaner under TP=4 because its four KV heads align with the tensor parallel degree.
- Measurements are direct 6-mer token generation, not FNS base-pair-level marginal decoding/scoring.
